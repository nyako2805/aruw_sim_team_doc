Regeneration

The regeneration class heals robots at a set rate, based off of the distance of the robot from the regeneration zone.

Fields

healInterval

int

The amount of time the tank has to wait before health is regenerated. Set to 1000 milliseconds by default.

team

int

The assigned team of the regeneration zone.

Methods

Start()

Is called when the game begins. Activates the regeneration zone.

Update()

Heals the robots in the regeneration zone. This method is called every second.
